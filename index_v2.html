Function of Barrage

The barrage reduces glass flowrate, enhances homogeneity, and improves temperature control.

  • Reduces working-end temperature
  • Increases bottom temperature in melting/refining zones
  • Blocks surface currents
  • Reduces energy consumption

Problem Statement

Water leaks in the barrage lead to 600 tons of glass loss, affecting production and quality.

  • Yield loss
  • Increase in special events
  • Production defects

Step 1: Initial Cleaning & Inspection

Activities included team assignments, technical display setup, and identification of abnormalities.

  • 7 Types of abnormalities
  • Tagging trends & register
  • Team formation and schedule

Step 2: Countermeasures (Kaizen)

Key actions taken to address abnormalities and improve system reliability.

  • Mini barrage installation
  • Flow sensor correction
  • Temperature gauge installation
  • Standardized penetration depth

Step 3: Standardization

Final standard procedures were implemented to sustain improvements.

  • Hydro & radiographic testing
  • CILT checklists
  • Visual markers & color codes
